04 — Patient Journey

Seamless End-to-End Workflow

Every patient touchpoint is digitized, tracked, and connected across departments.

1

Patient Arrival & Registration

Receptionist registers the patient with demographics and contact information. System auto-generates a unique Patient ID and Appointment ID. Department is assigned based on chief complaint.

2

AI-Powered Triage Assessment

Receptionist captures symptoms, medical history, and optional vitals. AI engine performs instant risk stratification, generates differential diagnoses, recommended tests, and a SOAP clinical summary. Emergency cases are immediately flagged.

3

Doctor Consultation & Clinical Decisions

Doctor reviews AI triage results, examines the patient, creates clinical notes in the EHR, orders lab tests, and writes prescriptions. Patient can be admitted as an in-patient if needed.

4

Laboratory Diagnostics

Lab technician receives the order with barcode tracking, performs tests, uploads results with remarks. AI-powered analysis generates a formatted report and notifies the ordering doctor.

5

Pharmacy Dispensing & Billing

Pharmacist receives prescription orders, verifies inventory, dispenses medicines with batch-level tracking, and generates invoices. Stock levels auto-update, low-stock alerts trigger automatically.

6

Discharge & Summary

Discharge checklist ensures all documentation, medications, billing, and follow-ups are completed. System generates a comprehensive discharge summary in PDF format for the patient's records.

05 — Security & Compliance

Enterprise-Grade Security

Built from the ground up with healthcare compliance and data protection in mind.

🔒 Role-Based Access Control

Five distinct user roles (Admin, Doctor, Receptionist, Lab Technician, Pharmacist) with role-specific dashboards and permissions. Each user sees only what they need.

🔐 Encrypted Authentication

bcrypt-hashed passwords with salt rounds. Secure httpOnly session cookies. No plain-text credentials stored anywhere in the system.

📝 Complete Audit Trail

Every action logged with user ID, username, role, action type, module, entity, timestamp, and detailed JSON metadata. Full traceability for compliance audits.

🛡️ HIPAA-Ready Architecture

Designed with healthcare data protection principles. Secure data transmission, access logging, and audit capabilities aligned with regulatory requirements.
